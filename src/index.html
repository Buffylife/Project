Merchant collaboration with the Hashly User
Community
A Merchant come become a member of the Hashly Sharing Economy by creating a Hashly
Campaign! Creating a Hashly Campaign means 3 Things:
  • 1. Honouring Hashly Credit

    Hashly Credit represents the total amount of money which a Merchant is undertaking to honour in Hashly Token purchases made at the Merchant's establishment once the Campaign is created.

  • 2. Buying Hashly Tokens

    After purchasing a Hashly Campaign, all Hashly Tokens are issued for all the User Community to earn. A Hashly Token is issued for every €0.01 of Hashly Credit.

  • 3. Experiencing the Benefit
    of Crowd Marketing

    Hashly will distribute all the Campaign Tokens to Users based on their original content on the Merchant's Brand and its exposure.
